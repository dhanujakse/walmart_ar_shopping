# 🛒 Smart AR Shopping Cart – Reinventing the Shopping Experience

## ✨ Objective

Create a solution that transforms both online and offline shopping into a smarter, faster, and more personalized experience — solving common frustrations while empowering customers with real-time, intelligent assistance.

---

## 🔍 Problem Statement

Traditional retail shopping feels outdated:

* **Inefficient In-Store Navigation:** Shoppers often get lost in large stores and spend too much time finding items.
* **Disconnected Planning:** Online lists and recommendations rarely help inside the store.
* **Missed Opportunities:** Customers pass by deals or forget frequently bought items.
* **Accessibility Gaps:** Shoppers with visual or physical challenges struggle with the standard shopping experience.
* **Frustrating Checkout Lines:** Customers often wait long hours at checkout counters, reducing satisfaction.

---

## 🚀 Our Solution: Smart AR Shopping Cart

**Smart AR Cart** is a connected shopping system combining **smart cart hardware**, **AR mobile navigation**, and a **web-based shopping assistant**. It brings your digital planning into the physical store — all through a cart-attached touchscreen and your mobile device.

### 🔧 Key Features

* **Smart Cart with Built-in Screen:** A digital screen mounted on the cart provides live shopping assistance, eliminating the need for external devices.
* **Augmented Reality Navigation:** Users receive real-time AR navigation directly in-store using their smartphones, guiding them to each item.
* **Voice Assistance:** Clear audio instructions enhance accessibility for elderly, visually impaired, or physically disabled shoppers, including wheelchair users.
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Context-aware product suggestions and live deals are shown as you move.
* **Self-Checkout Ready:** Billing happens automatically — no need to wait in lines. Just walk out when done.

---

## 📱 How It Works (Demo Flow)

1. **Build Your Cart (React Web App)**

   * Scan or search for products
   * Get live recommendations & deals
   * Tap “Navigate” to send cart to mobile

2. **Navigate In-Store (Flutter Mobile App + Cart)**

   * Use AR arrows and audio guidance
   * Follow a clear route through the store
   * Cart-attached screen shows list, deals, and progress
   * “Item Found” animations give visual feedback
   * Checkout happens automatically
